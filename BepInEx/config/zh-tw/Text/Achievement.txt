Restore 200 total poison by using items.=使用道具恢復200點中毒值。

Place 100m of rope in a single expedition.=在單次探險中放置100米繩索。

Climb 5000m total.=總計攀登5000米。
Escape the island in a solo expedition.=在單人探險中逃離島嶼。
Resurrect 3 scouts in a single expedition.=在單次探險中復活3名童軍。
Open 15 luggages in a single expedition.=在單次探險中開啟15個行李箱。
Escape the island without taking fall damage.=不受到墜落傷害下逃離島嶼。

Climb 50m upwards without touching the ground.=向上攀登50米不觸碰地面。
